Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal. It focuses on developing essential digital skills such as creating a professional online presence, understanding online communication etiquette, exploring collaborative platforms, and promoting cyber safety awareness.

Through this project, I gained practical experience with various digital tools and learned how to use them responsibly and effectively in both academic and professional contexts.

Tasks Completed
Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
An infographic was created using Canva to explain important topics such as digital literacy, safe internet practices, useful tools for students, and maintaining a professional online presence.

Task 2: Digital Portfolio
Profiles were created and updated on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and HackerRank to establish a strong digital identity and showcase learning progress.

Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
A beginner-level coding problem was solved on HackerRank to understand coding platforms. Additionally, a Google Form quiz was created to assess digital literacy awareness.

Task 4: Email Etiquette
Two professional emails were drafted—one requesting an assignment extension and another expressing interest in an internship. A checklist of social media do’s and don’ts was also created.

Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
A case study on UPI fraud was prepared along with a prevention checklist to spread awareness about online financial safety and cybercrime risks.

Conclusion
This project helped me understand the importance of digital literacy in today’s world. I learned how to use digital tools effectively, communicate professionally, and stay safe online. These skills will be valuable throughout my academic journey and future career.
